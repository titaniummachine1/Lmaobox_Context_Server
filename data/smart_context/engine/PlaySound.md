## Function/Symbol: engine.PlaySound

> Play a sound file from TF2 audio

### Required Context

- Parameters: audioPath (string, relative to tf/sound/)
- Sound files: see tf2_sound_misc_dir.vpk

### Curated Usage Examples

```lua
engine.PlaySound("player/recharged.wav")
```

### Notes

- Path is relative to Team Fortress 2/tf/sound/
- Use for notifications or audio cues
